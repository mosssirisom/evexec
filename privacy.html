Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This policy explains how EV Exec collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use our website or make a booking with us.

1

Who We Are

EV Exec is a sole trader providing premium airport transfer services based in Blackpool, Lancashire. We are the data controller for any personal information you provide to us.

If you have any questions about how we use your data, you can contact us at:

  • Email: book@evexec.co.uk
  • Phone: 07721 070370

2

What Information We Collect

When you submit a booking request on our website, we may collect the following:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number and/or email address)
  • Journey details (pickup location, destination, airport, travel date and time)
  • Flight information (flight number, where relevant)
  • Passenger and luggage information
  • Your preferred contact method

We do not collect payment card details through our website. Payments are handled directly and securely at the point of service.

3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Arrange and confirm your airport transfer booking
  • Contact you to confirm details, provide updates or follow up on your request
  • Monitor your flight (where applicable) to ensure accurate pickup timing
  • Respond to enquiries you send us
  • Meet any legal obligations we are subject to

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. We will never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

4

Our Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases to process your personal data:

  • Contract — processing is necessary to fulfil your booking and provide the transfer service
  • Legitimate interests — to communicate with you about your journey, monitor your flight and provide a professional service
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to retain certain information by law

5

How We Store Your Data

Your booking information is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider that stores data on servers within the European Economic Area (EEA). Access is restricted to EV Exec only.

We retain booking records for up to 2 years for operational and legal purposes, after which they are securely deleted.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss or misuse.

6

Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (e.g. our secure database provider), who are bound by strict data processing agreements
  • Where we are required to do so by law or a regulatory authority

No personal data is transferred outside the UK or EEA without appropriate safeguards in place.

7

Cookies

Our website uses only essential session storage to improve your experience (for example, to prevent accidental duplicate form submissions). We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies or third-party analytics.

You can clear your browser's local and session storage at any time through your browser settings.

8

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Erasure — request that we delete your data, subject to any legal obligations
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at book@evexec.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your rights have not been respected.

9

External Links

Our website may contain links to external services such as Google Maps or WhatsApp. Once you leave our website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their own privacy policies.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
