FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Preston to Manchester Airport transfer cost?

EV Exec charges a fixed price of £85 one way and £150 return for transfers between Preston and Manchester Airport. The price is fully all-inclusive — no hidden charges, no parking fees, no surcharges.

How long does the journey from Preston to Manchester Airport take?

The journey typically takes 45 to 60 minutes via the M6 and M56. Preston has excellent motorway access and it is one of the more direct routes to Manchester Airport from the Lancashire area.

What is included in the fixed price?

The fixed price covers: door-to-terminal collection, up to 4 passengers, standard luggage, airport drop-off and pick-up fees, live flight monitoring on inbound transfers, and complimentary refreshments on every journey.

Do you monitor my flight if it is delayed?

Yes — flight monitoring is included as standard on all inbound transfers from Manchester Airport. We track your flight in real time and adjust your pickup time accordingly. You will never be left waiting at the airport.

Which terminal at Manchester Airport do you use?

We drop off and collect from Terminal 1, Terminal 2 and Terminal 3 at Manchester Airport. When you book, simply confirm which terminal your airline uses and we will take you directly to the correct entrance.

Do you also cover the Fylde Coast and Blackpool from Preston?

Yes. If you are travelling from anywhere between Preston and the Fylde Coast, we can often accommodate you. Contact us with your pickup address and we will confirm pricing and availability.

What vehicle does EV Exec use?

EV Exec operates a Tesla Model Y — a fully electric, premium vehicle that is spacious, whisper-quiet and presented to a high standard on every booking. It comfortably seats up to 4 passengers with generous luggage capacity.

How do I book a Manchester Airport transfer?

You can book by WhatsApp, by calling 07721 070370, or via the booking form on our main website. Once your booking is confirmed you will receive a written confirmation with your pickup time and fixed price.
