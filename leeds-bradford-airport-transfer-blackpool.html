FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Blackpool to Leeds Bradford Airport transfer cost?

EV Exec charges a fixed price of £135 one way and £250 return for transfers between Blackpool or the Fylde Coast and Leeds Bradford Airport. Fully all-inclusive — no hidden charges.

How long does the journey from Blackpool to Leeds Bradford Airport take?

The journey typically takes 75 to 100 minutes via the M65. We always allow generous travel time and will advise on the right departure for your flight.

What is included in the fixed price?

The fixed price covers: door-to-terminal collection, up to 4 passengers, standard luggage, airport fees, live flight monitoring on inbound transfers, and complimentary refreshments on every journey.

Do you monitor my flight if it is delayed?

Yes — flight monitoring is included as standard on all inbound transfers from Leeds Bradford Airport. We track your flight in real time and adjust your pickup time accordingly.
