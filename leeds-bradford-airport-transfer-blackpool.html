FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Blackpool to Leeds Bradford Airport transfer cost?

EV Exec charges a fixed price of £135 one way and £270 return for transfers between Blackpool or the Fylde Coast and Leeds Bradford Airport. The price is fully all-inclusive — no hidden charges, no parking fees, no surcharges.

How long does the journey from Blackpool to Leeds Bradford Airport take?

The journey typically takes approximately 80 to 100 minutes via the M6 and M62. We always allow generous travel time and will advise on the right departure for your flight. We recommend extra time for early morning travel and peak holiday periods.

Which route does EV Exec take to Leeds Bradford Airport?

We typically travel via the M55 from Blackpool to join the M6 southbound, then the M62 eastbound through to the Leeds Bradford Airport junction. The route is straightforward with no city centre driving, helping keep journey times consistent.

What is included in the fixed price?

The fixed price covers: door-to-terminal collection, up to 4 passengers, standard luggage, airport drop-off and pick-up fees, live flight monitoring on inbound transfers, and complimentary refreshments on every journey.

Do you monitor my flight if it is delayed at Leeds Bradford Airport?

Yes — flight monitoring is included as standard on all inbound transfers from Leeds Bradford Airport. We track your flight in real time and adjust your pickup time accordingly. You will never be left waiting at the airport due to a delay or an early landing.

Can I book from Lytham, Poulton, Fleetwood or other Fylde Coast towns?

Yes. We cover the full Fylde Coast and Wyre area, including Lytham St Annes, Poulton-le-Fylde, Fleetwood, Thornton-Cleveleys, Kirkham, Freckleton, Warton and Preston. The standard fare of £135 applies to all pickups within the Blackpool and Fylde Coast area.

What vehicle does EV Exec use?

EV Exec operates a Tesla Model Y — a fully electric, premium vehicle that is spacious, whisper-quiet and presented to a high standard on every booking. It comfortably seats up to 4 passengers with generous luggage capacity, ideal for longer journeys.

How do I book a Leeds Bradford Airport transfer from Blackpool?

You can book by WhatsApp, by calling 07721 070370, or via the booking form on our main website. Once your booking is confirmed you will receive a written confirmation with your pickup time and fixed price.
