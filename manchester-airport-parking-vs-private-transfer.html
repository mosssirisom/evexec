When planning a trip from Blackpool, Lytham, St Annes, Poulton or the wider Fylde Coast, one question comes up time and time again:

Should I drive and park at Manchester Airport, or book a private airport transfer?

While airport parking can appear cheaper at first glance, the true cost often includes fuel, motorway driving, parking fees, stress, and the inconvenience of returning to a cold car after a long flight.

For many travellers, a professional airport transfer provides better value, greater comfort, and a far more relaxed start to the journey.

The Hidden Cost of Airport Parking

Most travellers focus on the advertised parking price but overlook the additional costs:

  • fuel for the round trip
  • M6 motorway traffic and delays
  • wear and tear on your vehicle
  • airport parking charges
  • walking from the car park to the terminal
  • the stress of driving home after a late or delayed flight

For families, frequent flyers and business travellers, these factors can quickly outweigh the apparent savings.

The Stress Factor

A holiday or business trip should begin smoothly.

Driving yourself to Manchester Airport means navigating motorway traffic, finding your parking space, unloading luggage and making your way to the terminal.

After a long-haul flight, the experience is reversed. Instead of collecting luggage and relaxing, you still have a lengthy drive back to the Fylde Coast ahead of you.

A private airport transfer removes that burden completely.

Why More Travellers Are Choosing Private Transfers

Premium airport transfer services offer several advantages over driving yourself.

Door-to-Door Convenience

Your driver collects you directly from your home, hotel or office and drops you at the correct terminal.

No parking buses. No long walks. No dragging suitcases across car parks.

Flight Monitoring

Professional airport transfer companies monitor flights and plan collections around the actual travel schedule. This provides additional peace of mind for returning passengers.

Fixed Pricing

With EV Exec, pricing is agreed in advance. That means no uncertainty, no meter anxiety and no last-minute surprises.

Comfort

Travelling in a premium executive vehicle allows you to relax, work, take calls or simply enjoy the journey.

For business travellers, the ability to remain productive during the journey is a significant advantage. For families, it means starting the holiday with less stress.

Airport parking may still suit travellers taking short trips with minimal luggage.

However, for families, frequent flyers, business travellers and anyone who values convenience, a premium airport transfer is often the smarter choice.
